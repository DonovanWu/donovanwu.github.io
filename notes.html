Sept 26, 2014

Watched Mahou Shoujo Madoka Magica: The Rebellion Story (the third movie in the series) with a high school friend this summer. Though it was a re-watch for me, after watching it, I felt myself like a typical Asian girl who has just finished watching some Korean drama...

Well, it's primarily because Homura is so hardworking... And she is so lesbian... and badass... I now admire her a lot...

The story is excellent, and the anime as a whole is phenomenal! It is some anime that makes you think hard after watching it. Actually I'd say every character has lived up to fans' expectation, including the matchmaking and all those stuff... However, things just don't turn out to be very good, which I think is something we should reflect - Is our expectation just selfish?
