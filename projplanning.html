Game Plans

  • Top-Down view shooter game plan

    Basic Concept:

    A Top-Down view shooter game featuring girls as main characters in some sort of a post-apocalyptic world setting, also including some general FPS game's features/concerns such as:

    • Difference of hip fire and aim-down-sight
    • Walking and sprinting
    • Weapons with various handling (auto-fire, burst fire, shotgun, etc.)

    Initially, this game is planned to be made with Flash AS3. However, I find out that Unity 2D may suit it better, so I'm switching to Unity 2D, which means I must abandon most of the work done so far and have a almost-fresh start. Nevertheless, previous development experience has definitely given me some good programming concept on making a shooter game.
