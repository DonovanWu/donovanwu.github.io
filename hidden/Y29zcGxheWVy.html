Cosplayer Pictures You Don't See Every Day

Convention:ComiDay
Location:Chengdu, Sichuan, China
Time:July, 2011
Comment: Alright, you will soon see that this is probably the most normal cosplay photos I will post on this page. XD
Disclaimer: I don't own this photo. It was downloaded from the web all those years ago, but I couldn't find it online anymore.
Partially, I'm putting this picture here to promote the city I live in (LOL). But also, for me personally, it represented a time when things were a little different from how they are today. Back then, Chengdu was not many years into hosting these conventions, and those who attended were in a more exploratory spirit. It was an exhilarating time to meet with people who share the same hobby with you. It was also a time when I remember seeing titles like "It's Time To Let Go of the GDP Complex" on newspaper... Well, guess things were at least a little different back then, huh.
Oh, and one more thing. So the two cosplayers in the photo were cosplaying Black Rock Shooter and... who? I think it took me six years or so to finally figure it out... It's Akemi Homura from Mahou Shoujo Madoka Magica. It might not be so immediately obvious since Homura usually has a more stoic look.
